Intro

Usually when we think about unemployment, we only count the men who are jobless and neglect those women who are sitting at home and have nothing to do except their house hold work.

We are humans. We live to be identified. We love to be recognised based on our work. Why only men thinks about money? Why only they make plans to increase the monthly income? Why can't women do something to support her family? These are the questions which are pushing us to make this initiative.

Work

We look for those women who do businessess at their home. We meet them and advise them on how they can expand their business.

Currently we are building a marketplace platform where all these local businesses lead by women can come online to sell their goods and services
